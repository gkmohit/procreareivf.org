Our Services

When you get right down to it, success is all about value and trust.

Your ultimate guide

Fertility does not matter, until you need it. Many women envision starting a family one day, but the unfortunate reality is one in eight females will have trouble conceiving or sustaining pregnancy. We take the time to educate you, so you can make well informed decisions about your body.

Causes of infertility

We aim to better educate you about the risks involved of postponing motherhood - declining fertility, increased chances of miscarriage and chromosome abnormalities. Thanks to medical advancements, oocyte cryopreservation is about to become the next most trendy thing amongst young women.

Identifying the right clinic

The success rates of IVF clinics depend on numerous factors. There are many other factors that are important when choosing a fertility clinic, beyond success rates. It’s important to find a fertility care team that meets your needs. Our job is to use our contacts to try and help you locate a team closest to you that can effectively tend to your needs.

Your counsellor, your friend

E-counselling follows the same fundamental principles as face-to-face counselling but uses a different medium. It provides you with an opportunity to explore personal issues in a private setting with your counsellor who will work with you to understand what you and your spouse are experiencing. Your counsellor is there to support you as your IVF journey unfolds.
